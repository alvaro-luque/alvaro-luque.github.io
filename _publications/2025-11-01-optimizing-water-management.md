excerpt: 'This study analyzes the water-energy nexus in a traditional, non-sensorized irrigation community under drought conditions (2020–2024) to identify management strategies that enhance irrigation efficiency. Electrical demand data from May 15 to October 15, when weather conditions are most unfavorable and crop seasonality drives peak irrigation demand, were examined using K-means clustering, which revealed four recurring daily load profiles each year.'

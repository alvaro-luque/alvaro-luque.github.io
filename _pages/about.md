About me
======
Hello! My name is Álvaro Luque Sánchez, and I'm a physicist from Córdoba, Spain. After my Bachelor, I completed a Master in Physics of Complex Systems at [IFISC](https://ifisc.uib-csic.es/en/){:target="_blank" rel="noopener"} (Mallorca, Spain), a joint institution led by the Spanish National Research Council (CSIC) and the Univeristy of the Balearic Islands (UIB); you can review some of the projects I did for my Master's in the [projects section](/portfolio). After doing my Master Thesis on Information Diffusion in Online Social Networks, which you can read in the [CV section](/cv), I worked as Research Assistant for the INSIGNIA project funded by the European Union and the Spanish Ministry of Agriculture, focused in the modernization of irrigation systems for the efficient use of natural resources. During this position, I published a paper as first author in the peer-reviewed Journal of Environmental Management, which can be seen in the [publications section](/publications). Currently, I'm working as a Temporary Lecturer in the Department of Applied Mathematics at the University of Córdoba, where I'm teaching Linear Algebra in unergraduate Engineering programmes and Advanced Mathematics at the undergraduate Business Administration programme. I'm looking forward to pursuing a PhD in Berlin next.

Research interests
======
My main fields of interest for research are:
* Statistical Mechanics
* Interacting Particle Systems
* Stochastic Partial Differential Equations (SPDEs) and Random Media
* Random processes in Complex Networks
* Mathematical modelling and simulation of social phenomena
* Computational Social Sciences
